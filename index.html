Cultivation And Harvesting

Cultivation: Coffee beans are planted in well-drained, acidic soil. It takes about 3-4 years for the plant to bear fruit1.

Harvesting: When the coffee beans start to turn red, they are harvested. Harvesting can be done by hand or by machine. Hand harvesting selects the ripest beans, while machine harvesting can harvest all the beans on the tree2.

Processing

Processing: After picking, the fruit is processed immediately to prevent spoilage. There are two types of processing methods: wet and dry methods3.

Wet method: The coffee fruit is washed and placed in water to separate the skin and pulp3.Dry method: The coffee fruit is dried in the sun or in a drying machine3.

Selling price

Coffee prices can fluctuate greatly based on many factors, including supply and demand, weather conditions, and economic conditions.
